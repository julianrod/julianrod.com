I was in charge of the design and implementation of the official website Argentina.gob.ar, that unifies under one strategy all service guides from the argentinian government and all of the official websites from ministries and official agencies.

Approximately 1000 pre-existing websites were unified under this websites and we worked with every ministry and agency's communication office.

In the span of 3 years we were able to unify all the ministries' websites and more than 1000 citizen services in a readable language and with clear steps.

At the end of 2018 the website had an average of 10.000.000 unique monthly users.

I led the design and development of Mi Argentina, citizen portal and app that works with Argentina.gob.ar, with the main goal of being the place where citizens can find their information and services in a centralized and customized way.

From this work a consolidation of digital documents started to happen, first with the national driver's licence, the disability certificate, the organ donor card and social security information.

It also paved the way for other advances, such as the digital driver's licence, other vehicle credentials and the digital national ID card.
